Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Date]

Overview

Lofi Zen Lab is a client-side web application that respects your privacy. All data is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to external servers (except for YouTube content, which is handled by YouTube's official APIs).

Data Collection

Local Storage: We store your preferences, playlists, notes, todos, and statistics locally in your browser using IndexedDB and LocalStorage. This data never leaves your device.

YouTube Integration: When you play music, YouTube's IFrame Player API is used. YouTube may collect data according to their privacy policy. We do not access or store any YouTube user data.

Analytics (Optional): If you enable analytics in settings, privacy-friendly analytics may be used (e.g., GoatCounter or Plausible). This is completely optional and disabled by default.

Third-Party Services

YouTube: Music playback uses YouTube's official IFrame Player API. YouTube's privacy policy applies to their content and services.

Hosting: If hosted on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel, their respective privacy policies apply to hosting infrastructure only.

Cookies

Lofi Zen Lab does not use cookies by default. No tracking cookies are set.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control:

  • Clear your browser data to delete all app data
  • Export your notes and todos via the app's export features
  • Disable analytics in settings

Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact the repository maintainer or open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.
